# Vellum — Brand Guidelines (v1)

A calm second brain for your markdown notes — it turns scattered fragments into
grounded, connected, citation-backed recall.

Reference point: **crystalline, not botanical.** Knowledge as pattern, fractal, and
symmetry — alive and generative, but rendered as math, not foliage.

---

## 1. The idea
Vellum sits quietly above an Obsidian / markdown vault — a calm intelligence layer.
You capture loosely; autonomous agents archive, decompose into atomic claims, connect,
and compose synthesis into citation-backed recall. Minimal, mostly headless: it does a
librarian's work. The notes stay yours. Voice: a quiet, gilded study — scholarly and
warm, never clinical.

## 2. The mark & icon family
A self-similar fractal lattice: nested diamonds climbing to one luminous center node
(the fragment that has linked itself into structure).
- Detailed render = dock / Finder / About. High-DPI detail is a feature.
- ≤ 32px = monochrome glyph (template image, single color, no gradient).
- Same DNA at both sizes: diamond lattice + center node.
- Because it is a fractal, the mark works at 16px, as a graph node, and as the whole graph.
- DON'T: orbital/mandala rings, recolored gold, stretched squircle, detailed render below 32px.

## 3. Color
  Vellum       #F4EFE3   canvas / background
  Viridian     #2F6B5B   primary brand
  Deep Blue    #1E3557   depth / secondary
  Gilded Gold  #C9A35A   accent (rationed)   (gold-deep #A8823C)
  Sprout       #3E9E82   active / in-progress
  Mist Blue    #BFD7E6   gradient light
  Ember        #C8743C   "needs your decision" — RESERVED
  Slate Ink    #2B2F36   text / interface
  Supporting:  Linen #FBF7EC · Parchment #ECE4D2 · Slate Blue #3A6E88 · Stone #6A6456 · Hairline #E0D6BE

Signature gradient: deep blue → slate → viridian → sprout (the blue-to-green field
behind graphs and heroes). Gold gradient is for hairline edges only.

Rules:
- Gold is rationed — lines, nodes, hairlines, one highlight per view. Never gold fills or gold body text.
- Ember is reserved — only "needs your decision." Its presence alone signals a waiting choice.
- Sprout means motion — in-progress only; idle/pending stay neutral.
- Light is the **default brand face** (icon, marketing, first-run) — the cream study is the identity.
  A supported **dark "night study" variant** exists (see `DARK-MODE-ADDENDUM.md`) — *not* an inversion,
  but the icon's blue-green world expanded, with cream becoming the ink. Dark is the night variant,
  never the identity.

## 4. Typography
- Voice — Spectral (warm old-style serif): names, idea titles, section heads, grounded quotes.
- Interface — Inter: buttons, labels, nav, body. Sentence case, never title case.
- Data — IBM Plex Mono: counts, confidence values, hex, timestamps.

## 5. Motif language (crystalline, not botanical)
- DIAL UP: fractal lattices, nested self-similar diamonds, tessellation, mirror shards,
  soft blue→green gradients, faint drifting node fields.
- DIAL DOWN: gold leaves, vines, foliage, sprouting branches, mandala/orbital rings.
  (These read garden / wellness / mystic.)
- Self-similarity principle: the icon, the Explore center node, and the whole graph are the
  same lattice motif at different zoom. For texture, use a faint fractal field — never a
  decorative illustration.

## 6. Motion & feel
- Ambient drift (20–30s loops) on node-fields and the connecting thread.
- Breathing, not blinking: in-progress shimmer is slow; focused node gently breathes.
- One exception: the "needs your decision" cue may pulse softly in ember.
- Respect prefers-reduced-motion (all animation opt-out).

## 7. Voice & copy
Intelligent, warm, unvarnished. Plain verbs, sentence case, no filler. The interface
speaks as the product, not a person.
- Say: "From fragments to clarity" · "Needs your decision" · "Supported by 3 sources" · "working quietly"
- Avoid: "Unlock your second brain!" · "AI-powered synthesis" · bare "Processing…" / "Error: failed"
Name things by what the user controls. Empty screens invite capture; errors say what
happened and how to fix it.

## 8. The product
- The Line — Capture → Archive → Decompose → Connect → Claims → Compose on a gold thread.
  Station color matures along ONE ramp (raw cream → composed deep blue). Pending vs
  in-progress as soft bars. Ember decision cue is the only attention-seeker.
- Explore — one entity at center, typed neighbors, confidence on every edge, never a
  hairball. Center node is a fractal of the mark. Grounded, citation-backed rail.
